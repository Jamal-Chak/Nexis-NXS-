# Nexis Revenue Streams Guide

This document outlines how the Nexis Network and its participants generate revenue.

---

## 1. Validator / Miner Revenue
*For those who run the infrastructure.*

### Income Sources
- **Block Rewards**: New NXS minted with every block.
- **Transaction Fees**: Paid by users to include data in the chain.

### Profitability Formula
`Net Profit = (Reward + Fees) - (Compute Cost + Electricity)`

---

## 2. Infrastructure-as-a-Service (Node Hosting)
*For MSPs (Managed Service Providers).*

### Business Model
Offer "Nexis Nodes as a Service" to enterprises who need:
- High-availability API access.
- Private network validation.
- Zero-maintenance blockchain infrastructure.

### Pricing Tiers (Example)
| Tier | Price | Requests/Min | Storage | Support |
|------|-------|--------------|---------|---------|
| **Dev** | Free | 60 | Shared | Community |
| **Pro** | $49/mo | 1,000 | Dedicated | Email |
| **Ent** | $499/mo | Unlimited | High-Perf | 24/7 SLA |

---

## 3. Private Network Licensing
*For Consultants and System Integrators.*

### Use Case
Setting up private Nexis chains for:
- Supply Chain Tracking.
- Internal Banking Settlements.
- Academic Credentials.

### Revenue
- **Setup Fee**: One-time charge for network configuration (Genesis, Validators).
- **Retainer**: Monthly fee for network monitoring and upgrades.
